Who are we ("ISCC", "we", "us" or "our")?

The Irish Society for Colitis & Crohn’s Disease (ISCC) is the patient-to-patient support group for people who are living with Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD) in Ireland. The ISCC data controller is XXXXX XXXXX, and can be contacted via email at info@iscc.ie.

What information do we collect?

When you download this app, to register we will collect your name, email address and assign you a user name.

  • we will collect this data when a user registers, purchases or uses ISCC services
  • we will collect this data through the app registration page

How do we use personal information?

We collect this data so we can register and add new members to the ISCC. On becoming a member, a person can:

  • avail of all ISCC services
  • use the full functionality of this application
  • receive support, advice and help
  • locate bathroom facilities
  • chat privately to other members
  • register for an event

The ISCC will use this data to:

  • set up members and register accounts
  • administer our membership
  • deliver events communication
  • deliver support, advice and help to IBD sufferers

What legal basis do we have for processing your personal data?

The legal grounds for processing your data is your consent. You can manage or withdraw your consent at any time.

When do we share personal data?

We will treat your personal data confidentially and will not disclose or share it with any other party.

Via simple toggle buttons, you can chose to be located or not. You chose whether you are open to chat with other members or not. If you chose to be located and/or open for private chat, the sole method of identification will be an anonymous user name, chosen by yourself.

Our approach to data security and the technologies and procedures we use to protect personal information are as follows:

  • to protect data against accidental loss, data is stored and encrypted by our web service provider XXXXXX who are GDPR compliant

We will retain data for no longer than reasonably necessary. Data or records will be retained as long as an individual chooses to be a member of the ISCC. If membership is cancelled by the user, or membership is not renewed, all personal data or data records will be permanently deleted.

Your rights in relation to personal data

Under the GDPR, the ISCC and this application respects the right of data subjects to access and control your personal data. You have the right to:

  • expect data portability
  • restrict processing
  • lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s officer

We will respond to and act on any subject data requests within 30 days.

We will not perform profiling or other automated decision-making processes on your data.
