The Irish Society for Colitis and Crohnâ€™s Disease (ISCC) is a patient support group for people who are living with Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis (collectively known as Inflammatory Bowel Disease or IBD). We offer telephone support, information leaflets, and organise meetings and events around the country. We also lobby on behalf of people with IBD for improved services and greater awareness of the impact of living with IBD. The ISCC successfully won the Social Innovation Fund Ireland Animate Award. This award will fund the introduction of the 'Living Well with IBD' self-management programme throughout Irish communities.
